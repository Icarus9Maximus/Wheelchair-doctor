About Us

At Wheelchair Doctor, we believe that regardless of physical limitations, everyone should be able to move both freely and independently. Our goal is to enable people with mobility disabilities by offering creative solutions that transcend the wheelchair and that go beyond traditional wheelchairs.

Founder

Our founder, Ernest Mongezi Majenge, was recognized by the Mail & Guardian as one of the ‘200 Young South Africans.’ This prestigious initiative celebrates outstanding talents between the ages of 18 and 35.

Charitable work

We believe in empowering communities. That is why we repair wheelchairs for Alexandra and many more communities' underpriviledged residents. By doing so, we enhace mobility, promote sustainability, and build trust with our customers. We want to see every disabled person having an access to wheelchairs. Together we're making a difference.

Our Values

Community of Advocacy

Beyond creating exceptional products, we champion accessibility and advocate for change. We actively engage with disability communities and raise awareness about the importance of stair-accessible wheelchairs

Innovation

We are committed to pushing the boundaries of what is possible, by constantly seeking innovative solutions to enhance wheelchair functionality, safety, and comfort.

Inclusion

We believe that everyone deserves equal access to mobility. Our wheelchairs are designed to empower people with disabilities, ensuring they can navigate staircases and participate fully in society. Inclusion is not just a goal; it's our driving force.
